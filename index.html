Features

Scalable Deployment

Automatically scale your applications across multiple Docker nodes with ease. Seamless handling of increased load, high availability.

Automated Service Discovery

Services can find each other without manual intervention. Simplifies networking and ensures smooth communication between services.

Load Balancing

Built-in load balancing for distributed applications. Ensures even distribution of traffic, enhances performance and reliability.

Rolling Updates

Deploy updates to your services without downtime. Maintains application availability during updates, minimizes risk.

Fault Tolerance

Automatically redistributes tasks from failed nodes. Increases resilience and ensures application uptime.

Service Health Monitoring

Continuously monitors the health of services and nodes. Proactively identifies and mitigates issues, ensuring stability.

Secure Communication

Encrypted communication between nodes using TLS. Enhances security and protects data in transit.

Centralized Management

Manage the entire swarm from a single point of control. Simplifies orchestration and administration.

Resource Management

Efficiently allocates CPU, memory, and storage resources. Optimizes utilization and ensures fair distribution of resources.

Easy Configuration

Simple and flexible service configuration using YAML files. Reduces setup time, easy to maintain and modify configurations.
